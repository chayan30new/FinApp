# Watchlist Feature

## Overview
The Watchlist feature allows you to track stocks and ETFs you're interested in but haven't invested in yet.

## How to Use

### Accessing the Watchlist
1. From the Home screen, click the **Watchlist icon** (ðŸ“‹) in the top-right corner
2. This opens your personal watchlist

### Adding to Watchlist
1. Click the **"Add to Watchlist"** button (floating action button)
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Ticker Symbol*** (required) - e.g., VAS.AX, CBA.AX, BHP.AX
   - **Name*** (required) - e.g., Vanguard Australian Shares Index ETF
   - **Target Price** (optional) - The price you'd like to buy at
   - **Notes** (optional) - Why you're watching this stock

3. Click **"Add"**

### Managing Watchlist Items
Each watchlist item shows:
- Symbol and full name
- Target price (if set) with a flag icon
- Your notes
- Date added

**Actions available:**
- **Edit** - Update any information
- **Delete** - Remove from watchlist

### Use Cases
1. **Research List** - Track stocks you're researching before investing
2. **Target Prices** - Set buy-in prices and check them periodically
3. **Diversification** - Keep a list of potential investments for portfolio diversification
4. **Future Investments** - Stocks you want to buy when you have more funds
